Founded in 2011, FullOnBaan has grown from a corporate training specialist into a globally recognized IT consulting and education powerhouse. With deep roots in Infor LN / Baan ERP, Cloud Technologies, and Digital Services, we partner with organizations and individuals across India, the Middle East, and beyond to deliver real business outcomes.

Our curriculum is crafted by active practitioners — giving students skills that employers actually demand. Our human-technology centered approach ensures every engagement delivers measurable, lasting impact.

Journey Highlights

2011 — Founded in Bangalore; started with Corporate Training for Infor LN / Baan ERP.

2013 — Expanded into ERP consulting, application support, and customization services.

2016 — Extended operations to the Middle East, serving clients globally.

2019 — Launched dedicated offshore IT support model.

2024 — Launched the FOB Ecosystem: five digital platforms for the global Infor LN community.
